What does Intentional Consumption mean?

I'm frustrated with modern culture. It makes it impossible for me to enjoy anything.

Our fast-paced world is always looking for the next hit: the next story, the next reel, the next big thing. We are overconsuming the content that is out there; we are overconsuming art and culture. And because of this, we are also overproducing art and culture to match this need for consumption. Masterpieces of art are replaced by low-quality short-form ‚Äúcontent.‚Äù Our modern culture values easily consumable, mindless content that is literally rotting your brain.

I've had enough of this! I don't want to doomscroll anymore. I want to engage deeply with the art I consume; I want to think about it and give it the proper brain bandwidth it deserves. I want to enjoy the things I bring into my life.

The key to this, I believe is mindful consumption (with intention). Take time with the material and don't jump for the next thing immediately.

This is my list of high-quality content the world has to offer. I wish to spend my time only engaging with the art listed here. I invite you to join me in this. Get a book, read it slowly, take your time to enjoy every word of it. Slow down with the input. Enjoy what you consume again!
